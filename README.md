## Features
- Get access to Meal of the Day and meal categories from the home screen

<img src="Images/home_screen_first_page.png" alt="Screenshot of the first page of home screen containing meal of the day and meal categories" height="600" /> <img src="Images/home_screen_second_page.png" alt="Screenshot of the second page of home screen containing meal categories and meal areas" height="600" />

- Look up meals by category or origin area

<img src="Images/meals_for_category_screen.png" alt="Screnshot of the screen that displays meals from 'Starter' category" height="600" /> <img src="Images/meals_for_area_screen.png" alt="Screnshot of the screen that display Chinese meals" height="600" />

- Get detailed cooking instructions paired with YouTube videos to help you follow the steps

<img src="Images/recipe_screen_first_page.png" alt="Screenshot of the first page of meal recipe that displays meal information summary, ingredients, and cooking instructions" height="600" /> <img src="Images/recipe_screen_second_page.png" alt="Screenshot of the second page of meal recipe that displays cooking instructions and a button to open YouTube video" height="600" /> <img src="Images/meal_image_screen.png" alt="Screenshot of the meal image in full screen" height="600" />

- Mark recipes you like as favorite to get quick access to them through the 'Favorites' tab

<img src="Images/favorites_screen.png" alt="Screenshot of the 'Favorites' section" height="600" />

- Search for specific meals by their name

<img src="Images/search_recents_screen.png" alt="Screenshot of the search section displaying search bar and recent meals" height="600" /> <img src="Images/search_result_screen.png" alt="Screenshot of search results" height="600" />

## Requirements
- iOS 14.5+
- iPadOS 14.5+
